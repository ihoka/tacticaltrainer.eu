**Last updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Tactical Training ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

**We do not sell your data.**

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

- Name and email address
- Account credentials
- Payment information (processed securely by Stripe)

### Health and Fitness Information

To provide personalized training recommendations, we collect:

- Workout data and exercise history
- Heart rate and performance metrics
- Training goals and fitness assessments
- Body measurements (if provided)

### Technical Information

We automatically collect:

- Device type and browser information
- IP address and approximate location
- Pages visited and features used

## Fitness App Integrations

We integrate with third-party fitness platforms to enhance your training experience:

- **Coros**
- **Garmin**
- **Apple Health/Fitness**

When you connect these services:

- We receive workout data, heart rate information, and activity metrics from your connected apps
- We may share relevant training data back to your connected apps to keep your fitness profile synchronized
- You can disconnect integrations at any time through your account settings

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and personalize our training services
- Sync data with your connected fitness apps
- Process payments through Stripe
- Send service-related communications
- Improve our platform and develop new features
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

- **Essential functionality** - Keeping you logged in and remembering your preferences
- **Analytics** - Understanding how our site is used to improve the experience

You can control cookie settings through your browser, though some features may not work properly without essential cookies.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Fitness app partners** - When you connect integrations (Coros, Garmin, Apple)
- **Payment processor** - Stripe, for secure payment processing
- **Service providers** - Who help us operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- **Legal requirements** - When required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest.

## Your Rights

You may:

- Access or download your data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Disconnect fitness app integrations
- Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or through our platform.
